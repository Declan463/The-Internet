Communication:

The internet is used to Communicate with people across the world or just a mile down the road. The internet has made it easy to keep in touch with your loved ones across the world with social media apps like Facebook, Snap Chat, Whats App, Instagram and Tik Tok. These apps make it easy to contact and keep up to date with your friends and families life. If you want to communicate for business through the internet you can use email, Microsoft Teams and Zoom.
